ABOUT US

JB Pro MX training is here to help you reach your goals. Our training school is the longest running in Europe, with more than 2500 riders that have participated our Wintertraining in Spain the past 24 years.

Simply put, it is something you have to experience yourself.

Pro MX training is run by double world champion, Dutch Champion, supercross Champion, and winner of the Motocross des Nations, trainer of the HSF Logistics Motorsport Team, Silver Action KTM Team, Honda HRC Team, Marchetti Team and coach of the MXGP Academy, John van den Berk.

His results as a trainer:

  • Vice World Champion MX1 2007 Kevin Strijbos
  • World Champion MX3 Sven Breugelmans
  • 3rd in the World Championship MX2 with Jose Butron
  • FIM junior World Champion 125cc 2005 Dennis Verbruggen
  • FIM junior World Champion 85cc 2000 Kevin Strijbos
  • European Champion 125cc 2006 Dennis Verbruggen
  • European Champion 85cc 2012 Davy Pootjes and 65cc Zdunek
  • Vice European Champion MX2 w/ Brian Bogers & 6th in the World Championship MX2
  • European Champion Supercross 85 CC and Vice World Champion 65CC Adam Kovacs
  • Grand Prix 250cc winner with Remy van Rees 1999
  • Winner GP Lommel Belgium 2008 Gert Krestinov

    • With a career of over 20 years as a former MX rider, and 24 years as an MX Trainer, John has been heavily involved in motocross for most of his life.
