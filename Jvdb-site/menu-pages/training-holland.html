TRAINING HOLLAND

Two time world champion John van den Berk will host a summer training camp in Holland for 3 separate weeks in July.

The price for 5 days of training (1 week) is €475 (excluding the track cost of €15 a day).

The summer training weeks are:

  • Week 1: 23 July till 27 July.
  • Week 2: 30 July till 3 August.
  • Week 3: 6 August Till 10 August.
