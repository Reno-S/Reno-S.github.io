John van den Berk organizes a series of training camps every year, this year being the 24th year. This year's training camp runs from the 25th of December 2017 till the 31st of March in 2018.


Surrounded by attractive gardens, Tryp Guadalmar features 2 outdoor swimming pools for adults and children. All air-conditioned rooms enjoy free Wi-Fi and views over the Bay of Málaga from the balcony.

The hotel has direct access to Guadalmar and Bajondillo Beaches. Guests have free access to the tennis court and indoor heated pool. There is also a gym available, though it is not included by default.

Tryp Malaga Guadalmar Hotel serves a breakfast buffet. The Cenachero Restaurant offers a buffet dinner with show cooking. There is a daily entertainment program and there are evening shows. You can enjoy cocktails on the terrace at Bar Picasso.

Málaga Airport is 3 km from Tryp Guadalmar, and the historic centre of Málaga is 5 km away. Horse riding, water sports and excursions can be arranged by the hotel.

Package 1: Training Week (Monday-Friday)

Price: €500.

  • Includes track cost.
  • Excludes the hotel.
  • Staying longer is possible, with a minimum stay of 1 week.
  • A stay of 4 weeks will get you 1 free week of training!

    • In case of an injury, refunds will be available as long as the injury happened within the first 3 days.

    Package 2: Bike Rental + Training Week (Monday-Friday)

    Price: €950.

  • Includes bike maintenance.
  • Includes transport from the hotel to the track and back.
  • Includes oil (Motorex), airfilters (Twin Air), and every bike has an HGS exhaust system on it, including piston.
  • Excludes the hotel.
  • Excludes fuel for the bike.
  • Excludes broken parts.
  • Wear costs (of the brake pads, clutch, tires, sprockets, and chain) are for the rider, and are calculated as a percentage of lifetime wear.

    • No money back guarantee on bike rental in case of injury. Training refunds are available in case of injury as long as the injury happened within the first 3 days.
